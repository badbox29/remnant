Terminology

Remnant organizes your writing into four nested levels. Here's what each one means and how they fit together.

Library

Everything you keep in Remnant. The Library holds every Corpus you've created — it's the whole of your collection, not a thing you organize directly.

Corpus

A major body of work — a project, an area of your life, a broad subject. The top-level grouping inside your Library. A Corpus holds one or more Scrolls.

Scroll

A section within a Corpus — something narrower than the whole body of work, but still a grouping rather than a single piece of writing. A Scroll holds one or more Remnants.

Remnant

A single saved piece of writing — what most tools would call a "note." The thing you actually read and write, day to day.

Cipher

Otherwise identical to Remnants, Ciphers are encrypted with a passphrase only you know. Even after unlocking one, the body stays disguised at rest — only a small window of real content follows your cursor as you read, so a glance over your shoulder sees gibberish, not your words. An "Illuminate" option exists for when you need to read or edit the whole thing at once.

Fragment

A passing thought, not yet committed to anything. Fragments have no title and live outside the Library hierarchy entirely, in Loose Fragments. Left untouched for 28 days, a Fragment fades and falls into Dust, where it waits a final week to be salvaged before it's gone for good. Edit one, merge it with another, or promote it into a full Remnant whenever an idea is ready to stick around.

Library → Corpus → Scroll → Remnant / Cipher

Any Remnant or Cipher can also be left loose — outside any Scroll — and you'll find it waiting in Loose Remnants at the bottom of the Library until you decide where it belongs. Fragments sit outside this hierarchy altogether — always loose, never filed into a Corpus or Scroll — and live in their own Loose Fragments section beneath it.

New Cipher

There is no way to recover a Cipher if you forget its passphrase.

The passphrase is never stored anywhere — not on this device, not in sync, not by Remnant in any form. Store it somewhere secure outside this app (a password manager, written down and kept safe) before you rely on it. The Cipher's title stays visible in your Library; only its contents are protected.
